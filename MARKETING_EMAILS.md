## Email Best Practices

### Sending Schedule
- **Welcome Email:** Immediately after signup
- **Tips Email:** Day 3 (let them explore first)
- **Story Email:** Day 7 (build relationship)
- **Newsletter:** Monthly (first Monday of month)
- **Feature Announcements:** As they ship
- **Re-engagement:** After 30 days inactive

### Formatting Tips
1. **Keep it scannable:** Short paragraphs, bullet points, clear sections
2. **One CTA:** Don't overwhelm with multiple asks
3. **Mobile-first:** 60% will read on phones
4. **Personal tone:** Write like talking to a friend
5. **Clear subject lines:** No clickbait, be direct

### Testing Strategy
- **A/B test subject lines:** Try 2 variants for big sends
- **Monitor open rates:** >25% is good, >35% is great
- **Track click rates:** >3% is good, >8% is great
- **Read replies:** Actual user feedback is gold

### Legal Requirements
- **Add unsubscribe link:** Bottom of every email (required by law)
- **Include physical address:** Your business address or PO box
- **Honor opt-outs:** Remove within 10 days (GDPR/CAN-SPAM)
- **Don't buy lists:** Only email people who opted in

### Red Flags to Avoid
- âŒ All caps subject lines ("FREE TASK MANAGER!!!")
- âŒ Too many emojis (one or two is fine)
- âŒ Deceptive subject lines
- âŒ Walls of text
- âŒ Multiple CTAs competing for attention

### Green Flags to Embrace
- âœ… Personalization (use first name if you have it)
- âœ… Value-first content
- âœ… Honest about limitations
- âœ… Mobile responsive design
- âœ… Reply-friendly tone
