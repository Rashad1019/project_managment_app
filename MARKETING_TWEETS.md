## Tips for Using These Tweets

1. **Personalize:** Replace [LINK] with actual URL
2. **Add visuals:** Attach screenshots, GIFs, or demo videos
3. **Timing:** Post at 9am, 12pm, or 5pm (your timezone) for max engagement
4. **Engage:** Respond to ALL replies within first hour
5. **Hashtags:** Use 2-3 max, make them relevant
6. **Variation:** Don't repeat exact format too often
