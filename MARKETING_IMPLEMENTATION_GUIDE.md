## üö® Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Launch Day:
- ‚ùå Launching on Friday (low traffic)
- ‚ùå Not responding to comments quickly
- ‚ùå Being defensive about criticism
- ‚ùå Spamming communities with promotional posts
- ‚ùå Forgetting to thank supporters

### Marketing:
- ‚ùå Using all tweets immediately (pace yourself)
- ‚ùå Only talking about features (share benefits)
- ‚ùå Ignoring negative feedback
- ‚ùå Copying competitors' messaging exactly
- ‚ùå Over-promising on roadmap

### Email:
- ‚ùå Sending too frequently (1x/week max)
- ‚ùå No clear unsubscribe option
- ‚ùå Not personalizing at all
- ‚ùå Walls of text (break it up!)
- ‚ùå Multiple competing CTAs

## üí° Pro Tips

1. **Authenticity > Perfection**
   - Share your real story
   - Admit limitations
   - Be human in responses

2. **Value-First Approach**
   - Help before promoting
   - Answer questions genuinely
   - Share knowledge freely

3. **Community Over Customers**
   - Build relationships, not just user base
   - Engage with other makers
   - Support other projects

4. **Consistency Beats Intensity**
   - 3 tweets/week for 12 weeks > 30 tweets in 1 week
   - Better to be reliable than flashy

5. **Document Everything**
   - Track what works and what doesn't
   - Share learnings with community (#buildinpublic)
   - Iterate based on data
